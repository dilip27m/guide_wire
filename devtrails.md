# 🚀 Guidewire DEVTrails 2026  
## AI-Powered Insurance for India’s Gig Economy

📄 Problem Statement Source: DEVTrails Hackathon  

---

## 📌 Overview

India’s platform-based delivery partners (Zomato, Swiggy, Zepto, Amazon, Dunzo, etc.) are critical to the digital economy. However, external disruptions such as extreme weather, pollution, and curfews can reduce their working hours and cause **20–30% income loss**.

💡 **Goal:**  
Build an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** that protects gig workers from **loss of income due to external disruptions**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Develop a system that:

- Provides **automated insurance coverage**
- Detects disruptions using **real-time data**
- Triggers **automatic claims & payouts**
- Uses **AI/ML for risk assessment & fraud detection**
- Works on a **weekly pricing model**

---

## ⚠️ Constraints

- ❌ No coverage for:
  - Health
  - Life
  - Accidents
  - Vehicle repairs  

- ✅ Must:
  - Cover **loss of income only**
  - Follow a **weekly premium model**

---
